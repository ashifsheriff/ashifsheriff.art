Planet Zero

Speculative fiction

The geometry of the city was absolute. No curve was wasted, no line deviated from its purpose. It was a planet designed for efficiency, not meaning.

Citizens moved in synchronized patterns, their paths optimized by the Great Algorithm. There was no traffic, no delay, and most terrifyingly, no accidents.

But in the silence of the perfect grid, someone began to hum a tune that had no mathematical origin.
