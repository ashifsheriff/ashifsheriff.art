About me

Clay fascinates me because it pulls me fully into the present. My practice focuses on the intersection of functional ware and sculptural forms. Created in my home studio, each piece is unique, bearing the marks of the making process.

When I'm not at the wheel, I write stories that often inform the narratives behind my ceramic collections.
